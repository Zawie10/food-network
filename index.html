Protein

Protein is a macronutrient that is essential to building muscle mass.Proteins are large biomolecules and macromolecules that are comprised of one or more long chains of amino acid residues. Proteins perform a vast array of functions within organisms, including catalysing metabolic reactions, DNA replication, responding to stimuli, providing structure to cells and organisms, and transporting molecules from one location to another.

Carbohydrate

Carbohydrates are the sugars, starches and fibers found in fruits, grains, vegetables and milk products.

Roughage

Roughage is the portion of plant foods, such as whole grains, nuts, seeds, legumes, fruits, and vegetables, that your body can't digest.roughage is the portion of plant-derived food that cannot be completely broken down by human digestive enzymes.
